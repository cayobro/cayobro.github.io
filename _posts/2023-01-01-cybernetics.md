Really honored that I became the face of the [Engineering Cybernetics](https://www.uni-stuttgart.de/studium/bachelor/technische-kybernetik-b.sc./) study program at the University of Stuttgart!

It is also featured on multiple websites such as [studieren.de](https://studieren.de/technische-kybernetik-uni-stuttgart.studienprofil.t-0.a-456.c-34710.html).
