I am a Featured Researcher for the 2022 *Women in Research* calendar!  

I was featured as one of twelve female doctoral researchers in a calendar highlighting early-career scientists from DFG-funded research consortia across Germany. 
The project shares personal perspectives and everyday insights into our research and life in science. 
It aims to increase the visibility of women in research and to showcase the diversity and interdisciplinary nature of modern scientific teams.
