EMBS Germany @ UCSD 🌴 🎓 

Nicolai and I had a fantastic time joining the IEEE Engineering Medicine and Biology Society Student Chapter Kick-Off Event at UC San Diego earlier this week! After a great introduction by Gert Cauwenberghs from the Bioengineering Department, we shared our experiences on how to get involved with EMBS and all the perks of volunteering. 

If you are a student at UCSD, check out their student chapter and the upcoming EMBS Conference on Neural Engineering in San Diego. Maybe you want to join the Student & Young Professional Social & Idea Pitch Competition on November 13th? 🧠 

A big thank you to Isgard Hueck and Gert Cauwenberghs for their involvement and effort.
